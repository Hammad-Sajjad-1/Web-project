📖 How to Play

🎯

Select Target

Click on a monster to select it as your target for attack

⚡

Attack

Press A or click Attack to damage the selected monster

🛡️

Defend

Press D or click Defend to reduce incoming damage by 50%

💚

Heal

Press H or click Heal to restore 25 HP (costs experience)

🎮 Game Mechanics

  • Objective: Defeat all monsters on the board to advance to the next level
  • Difficulty: Each level adds more monsters and increases their strength
  • Experience: Defeated monsters grant experience points
  • Leveling: Accumulate 100 EXP to level up and increase power
  • Health: Monitor your health carefully - it's your lifeline!
  • Game Over: Defeat ends when your health reaches 0

💡 Pro Tips

  • 🎯 Focus Fire: Concentrate on one monster at a time
  • 🛡️ Tactical Defense: Use Defend when your health is low
  • 💰 Smart Healing: Healing is expensive - use it strategically
  • 📈 Build Stats: Let your level increase your power before rushing ahead
  • 🌊 Wave Management: Clear one wave before taking on the next

Good luck, brave hero! ⚔️
